About Oor Saapadu

Why Oor Saapadu?

In Tamil Nadu, many people depend on local hotels and small mess shops for their daily food because they are affordable and easily available. Students, working people, and low- and middle-income families usually prefer simple systems that save time and money.

However, finding nearby hotels, checking prices, booking tables, or choosing self-pick-up is still difficult for many people.
